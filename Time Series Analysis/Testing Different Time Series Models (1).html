Performance Metrics:

  • Root Mean Square Error. Let $\hat{\boldsymbol{y}}\in\mathbb{R}^n$ be the estimate/forecast and $\boldsymbol{y}\in\mathbb{R}^n$ be the actual value, then the RMSE is \begin{align*} 
      \sqrt{\frac{1}{n}\sum_{t=1}^n (Y_{t}-\hat{Y}_{t})^{2}}
    \end{align*} Suppose y_hat and y have the same length, then
    RMSE = np.sqrt(np.mean(np.array(y_hat - y) ** 2))
